how it works

Nearish shares your approximate location (not exact coordinates) with a small relay server. The server checks if any of your defined contacts are within your chosen radius — then fires a nudge. Nobody outside your contact list can detect you. Location is sampled every 90 seconds to preserve battery, and never stored beyond the active session.
